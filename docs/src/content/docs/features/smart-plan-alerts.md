## Smart Plan Alerts

The Plan page now includes intelligent alerts to help you stay on top of your finances. These alerts automatically analyze your paychecks, expenses, and category budgets to provide actionable insights.

### Alert Types

#### Unmapped Bills
Shows expenses that are past due but not mapped to any paycheck. This helps you identify bills that need to be assigned to a paycheck for proper budget tracking.

#### Upcoming Bills
Displays bills due within the next 7 days that haven't been mapped to a paycheck yet. This gives you advance notice to plan your paycheck allocations.

#### Over Budget Categories
Highlights categories where spending has exceeded the planned budget. The alert shows exactly how much over budget you are.

#### Incoming Paychecks
Shows paychecks arriving within the next 7 days, helping you anticipate available funds for upcoming expenses.

### Configuration

Smart Plan Alerts can be toggled on/off in Developer Options:

1. Navigate to Developer Options
2. Find "Enable Smart Plan Alerts"
3. Toggle the switch to enable or disable

The feature is enabled by default.

### Visual Design

Each alert appears as a colored card at the top of the Plan page:
- **Red cards**: Critical issues (unmapped bills, over budget)
- **Amber cards**: Warnings (upcoming unmapped bills)
- **Blue cards**: Information (incoming paychecks)

Alerts only appear when there is relevant information to show. If there are no alerts, the space remains hidden to keep the interface clean.
